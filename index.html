Welcome to Armageddon 2025

This is a collection of player aids to help you with your Crusade

Player Guides

Post mission player guide - this guide allows you to copy and paste text easily for input into Administratum. It contians the Battle Scars and all the Battle Honors from the Armageddon Crusade book. For Battle Traits and Cruade Relics outside of the Armegddon Crusade book for your army see the Crusade section of your Codex.

Missions

Mission guides are meant to be open during game play. At the top half of the page, they have reminders for Agendas, Warp Events, Crusade Blessings and Unbound Adversary datasheets. The bottom half of the page is pregame setup instructions, the mission map, and the section to select Fixed Agenda's and Crusade Blessing that will show up on the top half of the page.
